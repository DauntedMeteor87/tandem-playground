# tandemclub — Design Language

> What a beautiful way to remember a moment.

tandemclub is about moments — a sunrise from a ridgeline, a first climb, the van
ride home. The product should feel like the *memory* of a good day outside, not
like a piece of software. Warm, hopeful, and a little nostalgic.

## North star: Impressionism

The visual soul is **Claude Monet** and Impressionism — happy, warm, optimistic,
full of light. Not cold, not clinical, not corporate-tech. Color and atmosphere
over hard edges.

- Reference: Monet — https://drawpaintacademy.com/claude-monet/

## Broken brushwork

Lean on **broken-brushwork landscape art** — visible strokes, soft blended
transitions, light that feels alive. Texture and warmth over flat, sterile
surfaces.

- Reference: Philip Taylor — https://www.philiptaylorcreative.com/p053/

## How it shows up in the product

- **Trip media & memories** are where this matters most — photos and recaps
  should feel painterly and warm, like a remembered moment, not a camera roll.
- **Palette:** soft, sunlit, optimistic. Earth and sky, not neon or grayscale.
- **Mood:** belonging, warmth, nostalgia. The opposite of doomscroll-gray.
- **Restraint:** atmosphere over decoration. When in doubt, calmer and warmer.

Apply this most heavily on the mobile app's photo and memory surfaces. The web
portal is a planning tool and can be cleaner and more utilitarian — but it should
still feel warm, never clinical.
