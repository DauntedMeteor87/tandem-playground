# tandemclub — Mission

The hardest part of showing up is never the trip. It's showing up alone. **tandemclub helps students find their way back to real life**— turning outdoor clubs and communities into an easy on-ramp to real friendship, so that showing up alone stops feeling like showing up alone.

## Why this exists

Freshman year, I felt trapped in a life I didn't choose. I was studying materials engineering, watching people get excited about viewing the  difference between ferrite and perlite through a microscope, thinking: *it's just metal.* 
I had no roommates I clicked with, no car, no easy way out. I could see my whole future unfolding in front of me, and it looked miserable.
What pulled me out wasn't advice. It was an intramural soccer team called "The Dads," led by a legendary captain, and a ski trip to Palisades Tahoe that got me through the winter. Neither fixed everything. But they reminded me there was another version of life available — one with real people I resonate with  in it. 
Unfortunatley there was not enough of those experiences that pulled me out, the identity crisis got bad enough that I dropped out spring quarter and ended up in psychiatric acute care unit. When I was there, I saw the darkest depths of mental disorders, specifically depression. That's why I'm building this. There are students and people out there right now who feel exactly how I felt, and all they need is a door, all they need is one stable friend. 

## The problem

We're more connected than ever, and lonelier than ever. Fewer close friends,
rising anxiety, a generation raised on online feeds instead of presence. 
Social media promised belonging and mostly delivered scrolling and time that has been mindlessy waisted. As AI blurs what's even real, the pull back toward real people, real places, and real conversation is only getting stronger.

## Why outdoors

Outdoors strips away the performance. You're not your major, your friend
group, or your resume — you're just a person next to another person on a
trail. That's when honest conversation happen naturally on its own.

## Who it's for

The freshman showing up alone. The student who feels trapped in the wrong
life. The one who wants to get outside but doesn't know where to start. The
club leader whose members already want this — they just need it made easy.

## The bet

Real friendships come from repeated shared experiences, not passive profile views. tandemclub exists to create the first real interaction — then the friendship, then the second adventure, then the third. What's on the other side isn't just a social life. It's belonging, and pride in who you were made to be.
